What is Moray Game Jam?

The “Best Game Jam in Scotland” is back, and this year is going to be the best yet!

Hosted by UHI Moray, teams will have 48 hours to create a video or board game based on a theme which will be unveiled at the event.

That's not all, there will also be a series of exciting workshops, led by a variety of guest speakers and industry veterans before the jam ends with the gaming playground where members of the public can try out all the games and decide who will be their People Choice.
