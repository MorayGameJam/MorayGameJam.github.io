Frequently Asked Questions

When is the Moray Game Jam?

The main event starts at 12pm on Friday 7th of March and ends 12pm on Sunday 9th of March, giving participants 48 hours to make their game.

During this time, you can also attend any of the workshops or social sessions.

Where is the Moray Game Jam?

The event will be held at UHI Moray, Moray Street, Elgin, IV30 1JJ.

There is a car park available on campus and there are nearby public transport links within walking distance such as Elgin bus station and train station.

What accommodation is available?

There will be a number of rooms available over the weekend for sleeping in.

You will need to bring your own bedding/sleeping bags, as UHI Moray will not provide this.

What equipment will I need?

You will need to bring all of the equipment you will need to develop your game, including your own PC/laptop.

There will be printing facilities available during the event.

Can you take part if you're under 18?

Yes. Moray Game Jam is open to anyone over the age of 16.

Applicants under the age of 18 must be in a group with at a minimum of one member at least 18 years old. However you will not be permitted to stay overnight and will have to leave the college premises by 10pm.

Will any catering be provided?

Various snacks and buffets will be available during the event for participants.

If you have any dietary requirements, please include them upon registration so we can accommodate for this.

What amenities will be provided?

There will be a number of facilities available during the event, such as tea and coffee making facilities, a microwave and showers.

What is the smoking/drinking policy?

No smoking or drinking of alcohol is permitted on site.

A designated smoking area will be in operation.
